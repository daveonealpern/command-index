Seasoning Policy
No conversion closes earlier than Month 24 post-acquisition (Month 36 preferred) absent written HCVT guidance. Dealer characterization and installment method eligibility under IRC 453(l), and recapture acceleration under 453(i), are open HCVT priority items. Deferring conversion benefits two to three years moves marginal units from CONVERT to HOLD; run this model with the seasoning delay in mind. Business Plan v8, Section VIII.

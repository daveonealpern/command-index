How this was built

Every retail order in your Amazon export from Jan 1, 2025 forward, one line per product, at its actual item price (unit price plus tax plus its share of shipping). Digital orders (Prime membership, app purchases) are grouped by order. Cancelled orders are excluded. This report does not check against QuickBooks at all, it stands on its own: these are the purchases your Amazon account shows, in the amounts Amazon shows.

Category tags are keyword-based on the product name, a first pass, not a certainty. If something is miscategorized, the product name is right there to judge for yourself.
